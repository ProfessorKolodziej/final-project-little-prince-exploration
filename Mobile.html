I'm very cold and dry but water exists in form of ice at my North and South poles. My surface has many craters, deep valleys and volcanoes. The largest peak on the red planet is a volcano called Olympus Mons, which is three times higher than Mount Everest, the highest mountain on Earth.

Unfortunately Rose is not on Mars, but don’t give up your search!

Jupiter

You just landed on planet Jupiter!

Did you know that I'm the largest planet in the solar system. The most famous feature on my surface is the ‘Great Red Spot’ which is actually a storm that has been blowing for about 350 years, if not longer. I have some of the largest moons in the solar system and one in particular, Europa might be able to sustain life in an ocean below its icy surface.

Unfortunately Rose is not on Jupiter, but don’t give up your search!

  • Equatorial Diameter: 142,984 km
  • Moons: 79 (Io, Europa & Callisto)
  • Orbit Period: 4333days (11.9 years)
  • Surface Temperature: -108°C

Saturn

You just landed on planet Saturn!

I'm a gas giant just like Jupiter, Neptune and Uranus. This means that I don't really have a proper solid surface as it is mainly made up of gases with a small rocky core. I'm the second largest planet and is known for my rings, although the other gas giants also have these kinds of rings, only my rings can be seen the clearest. Saturn's moon, Titan is the second largest in the solar system.

Unfortunately Rose is not on Saturn, but don’t give up your search!
